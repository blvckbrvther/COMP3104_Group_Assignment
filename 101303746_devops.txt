COMP3104 DevOps is a combination of approaches, 
tools, and a mentality for automating and integrating the activities of software development
and IT teams. The emphasis is on team empowerment, cross-team communication and cooperation, 
and technology automation. 
A DevOps team is made up of developers and IT operations personnel who collaborate to improve the speed 
and quality of software deployment across the product lifecycle. 
It's a new way of working, a cultural revolution 
with far-reaching repercussions for teams 
and the businesses in which they operate.
